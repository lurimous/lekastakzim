Who We Are

Infrastructure Built on Expertise

Lekas Takzim Sdn. Bhd. (formerly Hancheer Corporation Sdn. Bhd.) was incorporated on 4 June 1996 as a Civil Engineering & Building Contracting Company. Managed by professionals from diverse backgrounds, the Company has successfully undertaken and completed numerous Civil Engineering and Building projects across Malaysia — and now ventures into ambitious concession infrastructure in response to the Malaysian Government's national development agenda.

Our Foundation

Built for the Long Run

From our incorporation in 1996 to developing concession expressways within Iskandar Malaysia, every chapter of Lekas Takzim's story reflects our core values: quality, precision, and community.

1996

Incorporation

Incorporated on 4 June 1996 as Hancheer Corporation Sdn. Bhd., a Civil Engineering & Building Contracting Company, laying the foundation for decades of infrastructure delivery.

Growth

Rebranding & Expansion

Rebranded as Lekas Takzim Sdn. Bhd. and expanded expertise across roads, bridges, and utilities — building a network of trusted consultant partnerships and a seasoned management team.

Today

Concession Developer

Developer of the Pasir Gudang Elevated Expressway (PAGE) in Iskandar Malaysia — delivering privatised infrastructure under a Design, Build, Operate, Maintain and Transfer scheme.
