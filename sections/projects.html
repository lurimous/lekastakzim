Flagship Project

Pasir Gudang Elevated Expressway

Lekas Takzim Sdn. Bhd. is the developer of the Pasir Gudang Elevated Expressway (PAGE), located within the Logistic Triangle of Iskandar Malaysia.

PAGE will provide 2 lanes per direction above the centre divider of the existing Pasir Gudang Highway, significantly improving the Level of Service, travelling time, and vehicle carrying capacity between Johor Bahru and Pasir Gudang — the main industrial hub of Johor Bahru.

The expressway will form an alternative link between Johor Bahru and the Customs, Immigration and Quarantine (CIQ) Complex in Tanjung Kupang, Iskandar Puteri — implemented on a privatisation basis under a Design, Build, Operate, Maintain and Transfer (DBOT) scheme.

Location

Iskandar Malaysia, Johor Bahru — within the Logistic Triangle

Scheme

Design, Build, Operate, Maintain & Transfer (DBOT) — privatisation basis

Connectivity

North South Expressway → Pasir Gudang Port & CIQ Complex, Tanjung Kupang

Configuration

2 lanes per direction — elevated above existing Pasir Gudang Highway centre divider
